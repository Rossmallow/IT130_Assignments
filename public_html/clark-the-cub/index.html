Cubs unveil Clark the Cub, their first mascot

By Mark Gonzales, Tribune reporter

For the first time in their modern history, the Chicago Cubs have unveiled a new mascot.

The mascot, named "Clark," will make his first appearance Monday night in a private visit with children at Advocate Illinois Masonic Medical Centerâ€™s Pediatric Developmental Center. Clark will be accompanied by more than a dozen Cubs prospects participating in the Rookie Development Program.

The mascot was unveiled after the Cubs reviewed surveys and fan interviews that conveyed the need for more family-friendly entertainment. The comments included the introduction of an official mascot who works in the community and embraces young fans.
